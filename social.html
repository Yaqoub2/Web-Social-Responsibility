Voluntary Work

Volunteers participate in volunteer activities because of their sense of social responsibility, which drives them to do what is beneficial to society.

Donate now

Health

A study showed that: those who volunteer have lower mortality rates, greater functional ability, and lower rates of depression later in life than those who do not volunteer.

Engagement

A survey has showcased that volunteering boosts employee engagement and pride, with 89% of volunteers reported increased job satisfaction and 87% of them reported greater pride in the company.

Development

A survey undertaken by Accenture in the United Kingdom and Ireland has showed that volunteering develops people and fundamentally strengthens the business and 76% of volunteers said they developed core work skills.

Recruiting

Research from Cone Communications has showcased that 74% of employees find their job more fulfilling when given the opportunity to make a positive impact at work, 93% want to work for a company that cares about them as an individual.
